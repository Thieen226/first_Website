My Favorite

Horror

Horror is a genre that makes your heart raises. It contains jumpscares and sometimes killing. You will have a feeling of fear, shock and disgust as you watch this type of film. However, many people enjoy watching this, including me :). Because horror is interesting in a way that you have to be cautious about something is going to happen.

Thriller

Thriller often is about crime and detective fiction. It is a bit similar to horror genre. However, it will contain more mystery details and you have to investigate with the characters in the movie or series. This genre can exploit many different aspects in the real life world. This is a perfect genre for those who like detective and mystery stories.

Action

Action is a genre that a lot of people are interested in. This type of movie or series will have many fighting scenes and violence. Most of the time, this genre is about hero or the protagonist and antagonist are against each other. It is not recommended for those who are 16 years old. Since it may impact the children's bahaviors.

Romance

Romance also is one of the genres that have a lot of views. It mostly is about love stories between two characters or more. Sometimes, the characters in this genre will face many challenges before being together. It focuses on emotions and feelings between the characters. Many people watch it because they are happy to watch others happy.
